Case Study

Improving capital allocation under uncertainty.

Helping an infrastructure investor make more consistent portfolio investment decisions across risk, return, sequencing, and delivery constraints.

← Back to case studies

Context

Portfolio-level investment decisions required a more consistent framework.

The client was evaluating multiple investment opportunities across a fragmented portfolio of assets and opportunities.

Decisions required balancing expected returns with delivery risk, capital constraints, timing considerations, and market uncertainty.

Existing approaches relied heavily on static models and qualitative judgement, limiting comparability across opportunities and reducing visibility into downside risk.

Problem

The challenge was to move from isolated deal evaluation to structured portfolio decision-making.

Individual opportunities were often assessed in isolation. The client needed a consistent system for comparing competing uses of capital across the portfolio.

Assumptions

Investment cases were built on inconsistent assumptions, limiting comparability across opportunities.

Risk visibility

There was limited transparency into downside risk and exposure across opportunities.

Prioritisation

No systematic way existed to prioritise capital deployment across the portfolio.

Scalability

Existing approaches did not support structured evaluation at portfolio scale.

Approach

A structured framework linking financial logic, operational constraints, and scenario analysis.

We developed a decision framework that improved consistency, transparency, and comparability across investment opportunities while supporting portfolio-level prioritisation.

Standardise

Created a common basis for evaluating opportunities across the portfolio.

Integrate

Linked demand, cost, delivery, and timing assumptions to improve realism in investment evaluation.

Optimise

Enabled scenario-based analysis and portfolio-level prioritisation of capital deployment.

Result

Stronger comparability, clearer trade-offs, and improved portfolio control.

The client gained a more repeatable and defensible way to allocate capital across competing opportunities.

Return vs risk

Clearer visibility into trade-offs between upside, risk, and delivery constraints.

Consistency

More structured and comparable evaluation across investment opportunities.

Prioritisation

Improved sequencing and prioritisation of capital deployment decisions.

Strategic control

Better alignment between portfolio strategy and execution realities.

The framework now supports ongoing investment decisions and portfolio management, improving transparency, repeatability, and confidence in capital allocation.
