This month

Three recommendations for sharper thinking on systems, decisions, and growth.

Kept deliberately short: one book, one podcast, and one article.

Book

Thinking in Systems — Donella Meadows

Still one of the clearest introductions to feedback loops, constraints, and unintended consequences.

Podcast

Invest Like the Best

One of the better long-form interview podcasts for understanding how serious operators and investors think about markets, incentives, and capital allocation.

Article

Good Strategy / Bad Strategy

A reminder that real strategy begins with diagnosis, not slogans—particularly useful for work involving decision-making under uncertainty.
